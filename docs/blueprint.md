# **App Name**: EmotiTales

## Core Features:

- Emotion-Based Browsing: Browse emojis organized by emotional categories such as joy, sadness, anger, and excitement.
- Emoji Search: Search for emojis using keywords to quickly find relevant symbols.
- Emoji Sequence Generator: Generate an emoji story sequence by inputting keywords, leveraging a tool to decide which emoji best represents each keyword to visualize a short story.
- Visual Feedback for Story Generation: Provide visual feedback (e.g. an animated thumbs up or shooting stars) after the story has been generated, to increase engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant blue (#64B5F6) to evoke a sense of calm creativity.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue (#E3F2FD) to keep the focus on the emojis and text.
- Accent color: Lively orange (#FFB74D) to highlight interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a sans-serif font aligning to art and design; body font: 'Alegreya' serif to keep the reading experience playful and slightly stylish.
- Use colorful, expressive icons to represent different emoji categories, focusing on easily recognizable symbols.
- Incorporate subtle animations such as emoji bouncing or scaling when selected, and a short celebratory animation when an emoji story is successfully generated.
